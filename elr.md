# Public ELR Ranges — Where Can You Shoot Past a Mile in the US?

> **Status:** v1, 2026-05-29. Research-backed, claims adversarially fact-checked against primary sources (range operators' own sites). Access policies change constantly — **call/check the schedule before you drive.** Distances and fees current as of mid-2026.

## The question

> Thunder Valley is a public range that goes beyond a mile. What *other* public ranges in the US go past a mile — **no membership, no class, no match.** Show up, sign a waiver, pay a day fee, shoot beyond a mile.

## The honest bottom line

**The pure "show up and shoot past a mile" range is close to a unicorn.** Almost every US facility that *physically* reaches beyond 1,760 yards gates the far targets behind one of: a scheduled match, a private rental, a paid membership, an advance reservation, or a demonstrated-capability check. The constraint isn't the gun — it's liability and range safety. Sending rounds two miles needs a controlled impact area and an RSO who knows you can hit it.

Sorted by how close each comes to the true "walk-on past a mile" ideal:

| Range | State | Max distance | Walk-on past a mile? | Day fee | The catch |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Thunder Valley Precision** | OH | 1 mile (1,760 yd) | **To** a mile: yes. **Past** it: no | ~$30/day | Open Range days are walk-on to 1 mile; beyond-mile steel is match-only |
| **North Springs** | UT | 1 mile | Partial — gated past 1,000 yd | ~$10/day | Public county range, but ELR bay past 1,000 yd needs a capability check |
| **Cameo (CSEC) LRP** | CO | 2,003 yd | No — reservation + gate | varies | Advance reservation; past 1,000 yd needs ballistic docs or RSO accuracy check |
| **White Oak Creek** | TX | 2 miles (3,520 yd) | **No** | none (event/rental) | Match registration or private rental only — no at-will/walk-on access |
| **Coleman's Creek** | NC | 1 mile | **No** | none (members) | Private members-only; no day pass |
| **Immokalee Range & Gun Club** | FL | long range bay | **No** | membership | Long-range bay excluded from hourly walk-on; needs paid membership |
| **NRA Whittington Center** | NM | very long | No — training/match | entry fee | Open to public, but ELR distances run via training courses and matches, not walk-on |

**Reality:** if the goal is *literally* "walk on and ring steel past 1,760 yd with zero prerequisites," nothing in this list cleanly qualifies today. The two paths that actually work:
1. **Thunder Valley Open Range day** gets you walk-on to exactly 1 mile (not past).
2. **A one-time capability check** (North Springs, Cameo) unlocks past-a-mile without an ongoing membership — the lowest-friction route to *genuinely* beyond a mile.

## Tier 1 — True walk-on, but capped at ~1 mile

### Thunder Valley Precision — Kimbolton, Ohio
- **Max distance:** steel from 200 yd out to **1 mile (1,760 yd)**.
- **Access:** **Open Range days are walk-on — no membership, no class.** Pay the day fee (~$30), sign in, shoot.
- **The catch:** the **beyond-1-mile ELR targets are match-only** — they're not on the open-range card. Open Range gets you *to* a mile, not past it. If you want past a mile here, you enter an ELR match.
- **Source:** [thundervalleyprecision.com/schedule](https://thundervalleyprecision.com/schedule/) (operator primary). Community confirmation on [Sniper's Hide](https://www.snipershide.com/shooting/threads/thunder-valley-precision.7006294/) and [AccurateShooter](https://forum.accurateshooter.com/threads/been-to-thunder-valley-precision-in-ohio.4016487/).

### North Springs Shooting Range — Price, Utah (Carbon County)
- **Max distance:** public range with steel out to **1 mile**.
- **Access:** **public county facility, ~$10/day** — genuinely cheap walk-on for the main line.
- **The catch:** the **ELR bay past 1,000 yd is capability-gated** — you must demonstrate ability beyond 1,000 yd before the long bay opens to you. It's a one-time check, not a membership.
- **Source:** [carbon.utah.gov — North Springs Shooting Range](https://www.carbon.utah.gov/amenity/north-springs-shooting-range/) (county primary).

## Tier 2 — Past a mile, but gated by reservation / capability check (no membership)

### Cameo Shooting & Education Complex (CSEC) — Long Range Precision, Colorado
- **Max distance:** steel to **2,003 yd** (~1.14 miles) — genuinely past a mile.
- **Access:** **state-of-the-art public complex**, but the LRP range requires an **advance reservation**, and shooting past 1,000 yd requires either ballistic documentation or passing an **RSO-supervised accuracy check.**
- **Why it's Tier 2, not Tier 1:** no membership needed, but you can't just walk on cold — reserve the bay and clear the capability gate.
- **Source:** [cameosec.org/lrp](https://cameosec.org/lrp/) (operator primary).

> North Springs (above) also straddles Tier 1/Tier 2 — cheap walk-on to the main line, capability check for the past-1,000-yd bay.

## Tier 3 — Reaches past a mile, but NOT walk-on (members / match / rental only)

These show up on "2-mile range" lists, but **fail the no-membership / no-match filter.** Listed so you don't waste a drive.

### White Oak Creek Shooting Range — Mount Pleasant, Texas
- **Max distance:** AR500 steel reportedly out toward **2 miles** — the longest on this list.
- **Access:** **NOT walk-on.** Operator (Lonestar H+R Outdoors) states plainly: *"we do not offer memberships with at-will access to the range, but private rentals in addition to the posted dates are available."* Access is **match registration or private rental only.** It's private ranch land — directions go to registered shooters.
- **Verdict:** the "show up and shoot 2 miles" framing is a **myth** — confirmed false against the operator's own site. (This corrects the original premise in this doc.)
- **Source:** [lonestarhroutdoors.com/whiteoakcreekshootingrange](https://www.lonestarhroutdoors.com/whiteoakcreekshootingrange) (operator primary).

### Coleman's Creek — Ellerbe, North Carolina
- **Max distance:** long-range range out to **1 mile.**
- **Access:** **private, members-only.** No day pass, no walk-on. Range access is gated by a member calendar/reservation.
- **Source:** [colemanscreek.com/memberships](https://www.colemanscreek.com/memberships) and [calendar](https://www.colemanscreek.com/calendar) (operator primary).

### Immokalee Range & Gun Club — Immokalee, Florida
- **Access:** the **long-range/sniper bay is excluded from the hourly walk-on rate** — it requires a paid membership tier (Patriot/Sniper). The "up to 2 miles" claim seen on third-party lists was **refuted** against the operator's site.
- **Source:** [immokaleerange.com/long-range-sniper-range](https://immokaleerange.com/long-range-sniper-range) (operator primary).

### NRA Whittington Center — Raton, New Mexico
- **Access:** the Center is **open to the general public**, but ELR shooting runs through **training courses and scheduled matches**, not open walk-on bays. The widely-repeated "2,050–3,700 yd Spring Canyon walk-on ELR" claim was **refuted** (1–2 vote) — not a self-serve walk-on offering.
- **Confidence:** medium — worth a direct phone call to confirm current programming.
- **Source:** [nrawc.org/ranges](https://www.nrawc.org/ranges/) (operator primary).

## What "walk-on past a mile" actually requires — and how to unlock it fastest

The gate is almost never the distance — it's **proving you won't spray rounds off the impact area at extreme range.** Your fastest realistic paths to legitimately shooting past a mile:

1. **Clear a one-time capability check** at a facility that offers it (North Springs UT, Cameo CO). No ongoing membership; bring a zeroed rifle, a chronograph reading, and a ballistic solution. This is the closest thing to true "pay and shoot past a mile."
2. **Enter an ELR match** at a walk-on-friendly range (Thunder Valley). KO2M / NRL-style ELR matches are the path that range operators *built* the past-mile targets for — entry fee, no membership.
3. **Book a private rental** (White Oak Creek) if you want the full 2-mile envelope and can split the cost with a few shooters.

> With a .375 CheyTac (see [`cheytac.md`](cheytac.md)), the capability-check route is the natural fit — the rifle is overqualified for any 1-mile gate, so the only friction is the RSO sign-off, not the hardware.

## Caveats & verification

- **Policies change fast.** Range access, fees, and ELR-day schedules shift season to season. Every line above is sourced to an operator/primary page, but **call before you drive.**
- **"2-mile range" list sites overstate access.** Aggregators (Sniperology, etc.) list *maximum distance* and ignore the access filter — that's exactly how White Oak Creek and Immokalee get mislabeled as walk-on. Trust the operator's own site over any list.
- **Four claims were killed in fact-check:** Whittington walk-on ELR (1–2), Coleman's Creek guest-access terms (0–3), Immokalee "2 miles" (1–2), and White Oak Creek "50 yd to 2 miles, just show up" (0–3). Treat those framings as false.
